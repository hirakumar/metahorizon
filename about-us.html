Introduction

Spanning over a decade in the IT world, Metahorizon has achieved the highest level of technology and domain expertise to deal with the ever-changing and improving features in technology. Metahorizon’s transformative and agile services delivered on time and within budget helps our customers to mitigate risk and increases their competitive edge.We offer our in-house shared services, onshore as well as offshore managed services to ensure cost savings of our clients, leading to fast turnaround time, and increased ROI.

Our culture at Metahorizon is very unique. We are self-motivated, determined, and high-energy group of people delivering business results. Metahorizon has become a premier provider of information technology while creating a dynamic culture based on pushing the boundaries of innovation to its top. Our long-term relationships with our customers has given us in-depth knowledge of their business when leveraged with our technical expertise gives us the ability to live up to their expectation and standards.

Metahorizon Core Values

We are proud of the fact that throughout our growth, we have maintained the company culture that our founding members created and instilled and that is still reflected in each aspect of what we do today

Innovation

We approach business requirements with an open mind and seek a better way to fulfill client needs.

Respect

We treat our customers and each other with respect, courtesy and honesty.

Excellence

We strive for excellence in every aspect of our work.

Trust

We have a high degree of integrity and are accountable for the work we deliver.

Passion

We care deeply about our work and go “above and beyond” for customers and for each other.

OUR VISION

To create opportunities for others and be known as the company that empowers every team member to deliver an unparalleled customer experience. With this commitment, we will deliver a level of quality and service such that we become the most respected and reliable company to our clients, contractors, employees and the community we serve.

Our Mission

To catalyse our customer's growth with enriching consumer experiences and future-ready products. To embody a values-driven entrepreneurial and high-performance culture in our employees. To develop our communities into self-sustaining, technology-enabled ecosystems.

To Our Employees

To promote a culture in which we celebrate, have fun, and recognize those that work hard, help others be successful, and who deliver creative, unique, and unexpected experiences to our customers. We empower our employees to be leaders on day one. We are committed to being great at open and honest communication with our employees. We value professional development plans in support of individual and company goals. We invest in providing tools, training and experiences necessary to grow personally and professionally.

To Our Community

At Metahorizon, we feel it is our responsibility to be a part of the community in which we live. To participate, support and contribute to the positive wellbeing of those around us.

To Our Community

It is our obligation to completely understand our clients’ personal and business goals such that we deliver our services as a solution, not a transaction. We partner with clients who understand the value of the Metahorizon experience, and who are committed to our success. We value the success of our committed clients and work towards building a long-term relationship.
